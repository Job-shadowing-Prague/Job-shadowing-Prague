Job-Shadowing for Primary School Teachers in the Heart of Prague

Discover innovative teaching methods and exchange experiences with colleagues from across Europe.

About the Program

We offer a unique opportunity for primary school teachers to observe lessons, engage with Czech colleagues, and acquire new methodologies for their teaching practice.

About Our School

Brána jazyků Primary School with extended mathematics instruction was established in 2013 by merging two originally independent schools. We are located in two buildings in Prague 1 – at Vojtěšská 13 (for grades 1–4) and at Uhelný trh 4 (for grades 5–9). Our school is a faculty school with extended language and mathematics education.

Visit our school website

How the Program Works

  • Day 1: Introduction to the program and school
  • Days 2-4: Shadowing teachers in classrooms
  • Day 5: Feedback and networking

Program Pricing

The cost of participation in the job-shadowing program is 350 EUR. Funding is possible through the Erasmus+ program.

Each participant will receive a certificate of attendance upon completion of the program.
